Design Philosophy

Less, but better.

One product, total focus

We don't make a range. Every hour of development goes into a single formula, until it can't be meaningfully improved.

Nothing that doesn't work

No filler ingredients, no fragrance for fragrance's sake, no packaging that exists to look bigger on a shelf.

Honest by default

Full ingredient disclosure, sourced suppliers named, real performance claims. If we can't prove it, we don't print it.
